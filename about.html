About Koshalyam

Koshalyam Learning Solutions Pvt Ltd is a DPIIT-recognised startup headquartered in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Our mission: prepare India's organizations for the age of artificial intelligence.

Our Story

We saw the same
problem everywhere.

We built Koshalyam because we kept seeing the same situation — organizations purchasing AI tools, deploying AI software, attending AI webinars — and then nothing changing. The workforce could not use the tools. The leaders could not evaluate the proposals. The transformation did not happen.

The technology was never the problem. The human capability was. And nobody was solving it seriously — certainly not for government officers in Madhya Pradesh, or teachers in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas, or manufacturing companies in Indore.

So we built Koshalyam: India's dedicated AI capability development partner. Not a training institute. Not an IT company. A capability-building organization focused entirely on preparing organizations to think, work, and lead in the age of artificial intelligence.

"The technology was never the barrier. The human capability was."
How Organizations Must Approach AI

The three dimensions
of AI capability.

01
AI Awareness
The Challenge

Most organizations have surface-level AI awareness built on media exposure and vendor promises. Leaders make AI decisions without understanding what AI can and cannot do, leading to failed implementations and missed opportunities.

The Koshalyam Approach

We build deep, structured AI literacy across organizational levels — from board-level AI strategy sessions to department-specific AI capability workshops. Our programs are built on practical understanding, not theoretical exposure. Leaders leave able to evaluate AI proposals, assess risk, and make informed adoption decisions.

02
AI Workforce Capability
The Challenge

Even organizations that purchase AI tools find their workforces unable to use them effectively. The gap between AI tool deployment and actual productivity gain is consistently the most expensive problem in organizational AI adoption.

The Koshalyam Approach

Our AI productivity programs are role-specific, hands-on, and focused on measurable output. We train employees in the AI tools most relevant to their actual daily work — ChatGPT, Copilot, domain-specific AI — with exercises drawn from their real workflows. Productivity gains are visible within days.

03
AI Organizational Transformation
The Challenge

Sustainable AI adoption requires more than training sessions. Organizations need AI governance structures, updated processes, AI-aware leadership pipelines, and a culture capable of continuously evolving with AI advancement.

The Koshalyam Approach

Our transformation consulting builds the organizational infrastructure for sustained AI capability — governance frameworks, AI integration into existing workflows, leadership development for AI strategy, and measurement systems that track real transformation outcomes. We stay with organizations through the entire journey.
