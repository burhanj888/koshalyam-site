Government · Forest Department · Madhya Pradesh

AI Capability Program for
MP Forest Officers

80+ Officers 2 Day Program Bhopal, MP
The Problem

The MP Forest Department had significant operational data — wildlife monitoring, satellite imagery, poaching incidents — but lacked the AI capability to use it. Officers were unfamiliar with AI and skeptical of its relevance.

The Approach

A 2-day fluency program contextualizing AI within conservation workflows — satellite imagery analysis, wildlife pattern recognition, AI-assisted reporting, all using real forest district data.

Implementation

Delivered to 80+ officers across rank levels at the State Forest Training Institute, Bhopal, in Hindi and English. Each officer produced an AI-assisted operational report during the session.

Impact

80+Officers AI-literate and actively using AI tools in operations within 30 days.

Education · Teachers · Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, MP

AI for Teachers —
JNV School Network

20+ Schools 1 Day Program Multiple Districts
The Problem

JNV teachers across MP were aware of AI's impact but felt unequipped. Many expressed anxiety about being replaced by AI. No structured program existed for rural residential school teachers.

The Approach

A 1-day program addressing both conceptual and practical dimensions — AI's role in education, then hands-on training in AI tools for lesson planning, assessment, and administrative efficiency.

Implementation

Delivered across 20+ JNV schools in multiple MP districts, in Hindi and English. Teachers left with a personal AI toolkit for their subject and grade level. Principals attended separate AI integration sessions.

Impact

95%Of trained teachers reported using at least one AI tool within two weeks of the program.

Corporate · Multi-Department · Operations

AI Productivity for
Corporate Organization

120 Employees 3 Day Program All Departments
The Problem

120 Copilot licenses purchased — 3 months later, fewer than 10% were actively using it. AI investment was generating zero measurable return. Previous generic AI training had failed to create adoption.

The Approach

A 3-day immersion structured by role — Day 1 all departments, Day 2 role-specific (HR, Finance, Operations, Marketing), Day 3 advanced workflows. All exercises used the organization's actual documents.

Implementation

Cohorts of 30 trained over a single week. Each participant left with a personal AI workflow — customized Copilot prompts for their role. Follow-up session at 30 days to measure progress.

Impact

78%Copilot adoption within 2 weeks. Average 2.5 hrs/day saved per employee. Full ROI in 45 days.
